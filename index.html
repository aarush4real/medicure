Licences & Compliance

MediCure operates with full regulatory compliance under Indian pharmaceutical distribution law. All products carry valid batch numbers, expiry details, and are sourced exclusively from licensed manufacturers holding current Drug Licence, GST registration, and WHO-GMP certification. We maintain temperature-controlled storage and follow CDSCO guidelines for pharmaceutical storage and distribution.

Frequently Asked Questions
🚚

How fast is delivery?

Orders placed before 2 PM are dispatched the same day. Delivery within Chandigarh, Panchkula, and Mohali is typically completed within 24 hours.

💳

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD) for all orders, and UPI / QR-based payments via any UPI app including PhonePe, GPay, Paytm, and BHIM.

📦

Can I place bulk orders?

Absolutely. Bulk and institutional orders are welcome. Contact us on WhatsApp or email for volume pricing, credit terms, and dedicated account management.

What is your return/exchange policy?

Damaged or incorrect items are replaced immediately at no charge. Cold-chain products and opened medicines cannot be returned unless defective. Contact us within 24 hours of delivery for any issue.
